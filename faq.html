FAQ

When will the game release?

We're planning to release the game in volumes, and to release them separately. As such, we don't know when each volume will be available

What platforms will DREAMTONE be available on?

DREAMTONE will only be available on PC at release. Post-release, we may decide to port to consoles

What languages will DREAMTONE feature?

English and Japanese.

How much will the game cost?

Depends on the volume.

Will there be a demo?

Yeah!

What's DREAMTONE developed in?

Godot (pronounced as Go-Dough)

Where can I follow you guys for updates?

On our Twitter (also known as X) page, or on our Bluesky page.
You can also join our Discord server for all kinds of updates too

What are the minimum system requirements?

The game is developed on a middle-eastern shawarma grill, you can run it on almost anything that has OpenGL support
