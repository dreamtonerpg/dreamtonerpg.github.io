The hey's a DREAMTONE!?

DREAMTONE is a traditional RPG, set in a whimsical pastiche of the American 1980s!

Play as Nathan Bloom, your average American boy! Fight the likes of bounty hunters, street punks, devious demons, and even the ghoulish undead using nothing but your stolen baseball bat and traditional turn-based combat! Don't forget to call home though!

Be prepared to expect the unexpected as you talk to televisions and trees, some of which may even explode! You won't be alone on your journey though, you'll have friends and family to help you along the way!

But never forget, the sea will once again rise to sky.
